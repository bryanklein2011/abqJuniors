About ABQ Jrs
Youth Indoor Volleyball

The purpose of the ABQ Juniors Volleyball program is provide an environment where young women can reach their academic, athletic, and personal potential, both at ABQ Juniors Volleyball and after high school. We believe that through the values of the program, student athletes will develop the life skills to be successful.

In the volleyball program athletes learn what it means to have Wolf Pride and how important it is to compete with passion. They are taught mental toughness and how to cope with adversity during matches, practices, as well as in the classroom and off the court. When an athlete leaves the volleyball program, they should have developed a sense of personal responsibility and an understanding of the hard work that is necessary for one to be successful in life.

Our role as coaches is to guide student athletes through the development of these values by developing relationships with our student athletes that allow for trust and respect. We must be patient, supportive, and listen but also be clear and honest with our expectations and decisions. We also expect to perform well in matches and push the student athlete out of their comfort zones to achieve success. We know the hard work that is necessary to have a successful program and we are dedicated to pushing our athletes to those limits.

Teams We Offer
Local

Just starting volleyball? Want to develop your skills? Our local program is based on the belief of building a true love for the game. This program is a development program for club volleyball. Beginners, ages 14 and under can be part of a local program to help develop skills and learn how to be a part of a team. Another great part of this program is flexibility. The team(s) will be strictly local. Practice will be held once a week and scrimmages will be held directly after practice!

$200 Monthly Includes Club T-Shirt

Regional

Regional level teams are created to begin travelling to tournaments within the region. The next step to local teams allowing for competition with other clubs. Regional teams will travel as far as Amarillo, Lubbock, El Paso, and Durango.

$2000 Per Season December 1st-April 30th Jersey Package not Included

National

National National programs are the top tier in the club volleyball world. This will be our most competitive teams and teams that travel to National Qualifier tournaments in pursuit of earning a bid to Nationals! These teams play in some regional tournaments and will travel to places like Phoenix, Dallas, Reno, Orlando, Salt Lake City, Irvine, and Denver.

$3000 Per Season December 1st-April 30th Jersey Package not Included
